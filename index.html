Mitchel Hunter | WDD 130

Hey guys! My name is Mitchel Hunter. I love hiking and photography. My favorite places are in the mountians and next to waterfalls. If I'm out there, I probably have a camera with me. I find joy in bringing the beauty of this world to others through photos.

When I look at a photo, my first thought is always about what I am feeling. Does it make me feel anything? In my opinion, art should always stir up something in the viewer. It doesn't matter a whole lot what it is so long as the viewer feels something.

I have lived with depression for most of my adult life. During those lows, art is something that has helped me through it. When I feel numb, sometimes a photo, song, or painting can reach into me and help me remember what it feels like to be alive again.
